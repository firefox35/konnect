Welcome to Konnect!

  1. Please enter your name in the box.
  2. Click the Submit button.
  3. This will bring to the main game.
  4. Enter your five letter word guess in the box and then click with the enter button.
  5. Once Clicked the game timer starts. You have alotted time to complete the game before the time runs out.
  6. Once entered your word, it then goes off to checks it against the computer word and return either with three different response colours
  7. The "Green!" colour refers to the right position in the box.
  8. The "Orange!" colour refers to the right letter in the wrong position in the box.
  9. The "Red!" colour refers to the letter is not part of the game.
  10. The game is get the word before time runs in the fast time.
  11. You time with be show at the end of the game, if you complete the task in time.
  12. Good Luck!
