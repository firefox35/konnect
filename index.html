Welcome to Konnect!

  1. The objective of the game is to get the correct answer before your lives run out.
  2. Please enter your name in the textbox.
  3. Your username must be more than 2 letters to be valid otherwise an alert will be displayed.
  4. Click the Submit button.
  5. This will bring you to the main game.
  6. Please enter your 5 letter word guess in the textbox and then click the submit button.
  7. Each time you enter a word your lives will decrease, till either you run out of guesses or you get it correct.
  8. You have five attempts before the game is over.
  9. Once you have entered your word, it then compares your word against the correct answer.
  10. It will then return 3 different response colours.
  11. The "Green!" colour refers to the right position in the box.
  12. The "Orange!" colour refers to the right letter in the wrong position in the box.
  13. The "Red!" colour refers to the letter is not part of the game.
  14. If you have guessed the word correctly it will display all the letters green and your name as a winner.
  15. If you have run out of lives and your guess is wrong it will display the correct answer to the game.
  16. You can end the game by clicking the home or the play again button both will bring you back to the introduction screen.
  17. Good Luck!
