Welcome to Konnect!

  1. The objective of the game is to get the correct answer before your lives run out.
  2. Please enter your name in the textbox.
  3. Click the Submit button.
  4. This will bring you to the main game.
  5. Please enter your 5 letter word guess in the textbox and then click the submit button.
  6. Each time you enter a word your lives will decrease, till either you run out of guesses or you get it correct.
  7. You have five attempts before the game is over.
  8. Once you have entered your word, it then compares your word against the correct answer.
  9. It will then return 3 different response colours.
  10. The "Green!" color refers to the right position in the box.
  11. The "Orange!" color refers to the right letter in the wrong position in the box.
  12. The "Red!" color refers to the letter is not part of the game.
  14. When you have run out of live and your guess is wrong it will displ the end of the game, if you complete the task in time.
  15. Good Luck!
